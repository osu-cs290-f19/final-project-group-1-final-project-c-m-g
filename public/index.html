Lasagna**Modal Recipe Card

Cook Time:

90 Minutes

Serves:

10 People

Difficulty:

Easy

Ingredients:
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb sausage crumbles
  • 16 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 Tbsp each: Italian herb blend, red chili flakes, salt, pepper
  • 1 pint ricotta
  • 1 Tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 lb mozzarella

  1. Instructions:
    Brown sausage, set aside. Sautee onions and garlic in the same pan, add herbs and spices, tomato sauce, simmer.
    Mix cinnamon and ricotta, shred mozzarella.

  2. Instructions:
    Brown sausage, set aside. Sautee onions and garlic in the same pan, add herbs and spices. add tomato sauce, simmer.
    Build layers in this order:
    Sauce, noodles, ricotta, sausage, mozzarella, repeat.
